HR and recruiting teams use Dust to answer recurring policy questions, coach managers on written feedback, screen resumes, and draft job descriptions and interview notes.

| Use Case                         | Task to tackle                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| -------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| HR Inquiry and Policy Assistance | Automatically answer questions the People team gets most regularly about company processes and policies                                                                                                    |
| Manager Co-Pilot                 | Coach managers in their daily tasks, helping refine written feedback, and assessing its quality based on your own guidelines and external best practices.                                                  |
| Recruiter Co-Pilot               | Slash the time it takes recruiters to screen resumes, generate external communications, job descriptions, or build skill assessments and tailored interview questions based on your internal HR knowledge. |
| Interviewer Co-Pilot             | Organize, correct, and translate hiring interview notes, in a few seconds rather than minutes.                                                                                                             |
