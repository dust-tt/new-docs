In Dust, you own and control all your business data. We don't train models on your company data, and your usage doesn't affect how our models learn. Our platform is SOC 2 compliant, with all conversations encrypted both in transit and at rest.

With the admin console, managing team members is simple. You can also use domain verification, SSO, and access usage insights.

## Enterprise-grade security and privacy

- Customer prompts and company data are not used for training models.
- Data encryption at rest (AES 256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
- Certified SOC 2 compliant
- GDPR compliant

**For more information, please visit our [Trust Center](https://trust.dust.com) or contact us.**
