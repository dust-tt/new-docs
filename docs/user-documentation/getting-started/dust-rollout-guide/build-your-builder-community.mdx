---
title: "Build your builder community"
---


A strong builder community makes Dust adoption stick. Here's how to identify, train, and support your builders.

## Who should be a builder?

### Ideal candidates

- Employees with a deep understanding of their team workflow and know-how AND willing to learn new AI skills
- Department leads with efficiency objectives
- Anyone passionate about improving team efficiency

### Key qualities

- Problem-solving and/or tinkerer mindset
- Understanding of department workflows
- Willingness to share knowledge and help other team members

## Building your builders community

### 1. Identify builders

- Reach out directly to people you know could be interested or run a survey to identify who uses LLMs regularly
- Look across all departments or identify key teams depending on your chosen strategy
- Focus on enthusiasm and potential
- Welcome builders at all levels

### 2. Create builder hub

- Dedicated Slack channel
- Regular builder meetups
- Create a knowledge sharing and template library hub

### 3. Train and support builders

- Provide training - train the trainer
- Share best practices
- Offer technical support
- Recognize achievements
- Ask the Dust team for support on this

## Builder responsibilities

- Create team-specific agents
- Document use cases and templates
- Train team members
- Share success stories
- Gather feedback
- Share feedback with the Dust team
