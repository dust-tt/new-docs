# How to Change Your Email Address in Dust

**1. Invite Your New Email Address**\
First, invite your new email address to your Dust workspace. You can do this from the workspace settings or by asking a workspace administrator to send an invitation to your new email.

**2. Accept the Invitation**\
Check your new email inbox for the Dust invitation. Click the invitation link and join the workspace using your new email address. You'll now have access to the workspace with both your old and new email addresses.

**3. Remove Your Old Email**\
Once you've successfully joined with your new email, revoke access for your old email address. Go to the workspace member settings and remove the user account associated with your previous email. If you are not an admin, you can ask your Dust admin to revoke your old email's account. 

## Important Notes

* Make sure you can access your new email address before starting this process
* If you have any agents created with the old email account, make sure to make your new email's account an editor of those agents as well, otherwise you might risk not able to edit them
* Consider informing your team about the email change to avoid confusion

<br />

That's it! Your email address change is complete, and you can continue using Dust with your new email credentials. If you have any questions about this, feel free to drop us a line at [support@dust.tt](mailto:support@dust.tt)
